Tiramisu recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500 gr mascarpone
  • 6 eggs
  • 150 gr sugar
  • 1 package (400 gr) of ladyfinger biscuit (savoiardi)
  • a couple of spoon of some liquor (rhum for example)
  • 2 mugs of strong coffee
  • cocoa powder

Preparation:

  1. Beat egg yolks and sugar in a bowl using a whisk or an electric mixer at medium speed for few minutes until the sugar melt and the cream became almost white.
  2. Add the mascarpone and the liquor and mix again.
  3. Prepare the coffee.
  4. Mix the egg whites with the whisk or the electric mixer until they hold still, then gently add the egg whites with the yolk-sugar-mascarpone cream, without mixing, but incorporating slowly to not unmount the white.
  5. Put part of the coffee in a plate and soak the biscuits in the coffee rotating them quickly, then dispose the biscuits in a large dish.
  6. When the first layer of biscuits is over, put half of the cream on top and gently spread it on all the surface.
  7. Spread some cocoa powder on top of the layer.
  8. Repeat the procedure with the rest of the biscuits to do a second layer, then add the rest of the cream and finish with cocoa powder.
  9. Let rest at least a night.

Enjoy!
